Best Hot Cocoa

home

ingredients

1. storebought Hershey's cocoa 1/4 cup i've modified the recipe from the box

2. sugar 1/4 cups

3. milk 4 cups

4. hot water 1/3 cup

5. vanilla extract four drops

6. salt a dash

7. cinnamon a pinch in your cup

you will need

1. ceramic pot

2. measuring spoon measuring cups work too

3. stirring spatula

steps

lets get cooking

1. mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in a pot

2. stir in the hot water on medium heat

3. once boiled stir for one minute

4. add milk and stir bring down the heat, we don't want anymore boiling

5. remove from heat, add vanilla and cinnamon

best enjoyed on a cold night
