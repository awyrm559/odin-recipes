Delicious Mixed Sauce Pasta

home

ingredients

mushroom i use beech mushrooms but any will do

onion one, diced finely

bell peppers choose any color you like

garlic two cloves, dice these as well

ground beef or pork 200g, you can choose another protein you like or substitute with a tofu product

bacon the more the better

pasta of your choice i use penne or fusili because they are the most fun to eat

pasta sauce two jars,i use a meat and a cheese sauce

salt a dash cooking oil, condiments sriracha, ketchup, cheese whatever else you like

you will need

  • frying pan or pot non stick is always good
  • kitchen knife knife should be sharp
  • wooden spatula

    • steps

    the vegetables

    peel and dice garlic

    peel onions, and dice into small squares

    wash mushrooms well and cut into small bite sized pieces

    wash bell peppers, remove stem, clean out seeds and cut into strips

    the proteins

    chop bacon into squares

    lets get cooking

  • pre-heat your frying pan on medium heat, wait about 1-2 minutes and add oil to coat the pan
  • add garlic, onions, bacon and stir the room should start smelling really good now
  • add mushrooms, ground meat or tofu, and bell peppers mix well turn down to low heat and cover
  • at this time you can start boiling some pasta with a pinch of salt i like chewy pasta so around 7 minutes
  • go back to your pan or pot, all the ingredients should be cooked through if they aren't, give them a little more time
  • add two jars pasta sauce of your choice turn to high heat and mix well
  • once sauce starts to bubble a little turn heat to low and allow simmer for 1 minute
  • when pasta is ready remove and plate, cover with desired amount of sauce
